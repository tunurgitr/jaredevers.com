Let Go and Forget

      Primarily written in Southern California, you'll notice a lot of references and tonalities that take you to the Golden State. Each song is written out of a real experience, and much heartache and love.

      A wide variety is heard between songs of hopeful love, like "How to Love" and "Backwards Forwards," to melancholy emotion, like "All We Know," and "Let Go and Forget." Simple instrumentation with intricate layering make for an easy yet enticing listen, with smooth melodies and lyrics to fit the pieces together.

      Because of the way it was written and compiled, this album is best listened to in one sitting.

      Jared is a singer/songwriter from Portland, Oregon, who combines simplistic songwriting with honest lyrics and creative tones. His writing is real and true, and depicts stories from his own life and the lives of those near him. Simple instrumentation with intricate layering make for an easy yet enticing listen, with smooth melodies and lyrics to fit the pieces together.

      Jared currently lives with Nashville, Tennessee, with his brother, Joel Evers (drums), and his friend, Joseph Starr (guitar). Joel is an avid photographer when he's not drumming, and you can view his inspiring photographs on Instagram, Twitter, and VSCO through the handle @joelgonewild. Joseph uses most of his time to improve his tone, so he doesn't bother with things like tweets and instas. You could probably write him a good-old-fashioned letter if you really wanted to.

      As a lover of all people, Jared enjoys speaking to new people and hearing stories from all over. Follow or contact him through his social media links below, or give him a direct email at jaredeversmusic@gmail.com.
